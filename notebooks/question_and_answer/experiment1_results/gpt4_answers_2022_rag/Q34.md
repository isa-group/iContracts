The customer did not pay the previous fee. How much should the customer pay this month?
## Background
The user question pertains to the calculation of payment due by a customer for a provider's services under an annual subscription model. The customer has not paid the previous fee, and the total amount of the fees for the annual subscription is $528. The user is asking for the amount that the customer should pay for the current month, taking into account any applicable late payment charges as per the provider's terms of service.

## Answer
- The customer should pay the annual fee of $528 plus 2% monthly interest on the past due amount. Assuming the payment is one month overdue, the interest for one month would be 2% of $528, which is $10.56. Therefore, the total amount the customer should pay this month is $528 + $10.56 = $538.56.
